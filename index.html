about

Building off of a lifelong love of jazz and blues, Glitch Gatsby brings together an anachronistic fusion of brass and bass, with live saxophone and visuals.

Now ten years into performing, he holds residencies with Detroit's esteemed Theatre Bizarre and Ybor City, Tampa's biannual Steampunk Ball, as well as various spots at Electric Forest music festival, including the speakeasy and silent disco.

about me

services

DJing

With nearly a decade's experience on the decks and about half as long touring, Glitch Gatsby effortlessly spins together a genre-defying sonic journey of jazz, blues, heavy bass, and midtempo

saxophone

His guitar stolen while vagabonding, Glitch Gatsby pivoted to saxophone, translating his blues and funk vocabulary to the new instrument. Now, it's a cornerstone of his live stage sound.

live visuals

With cutting-edge projection software, Glitch Gatsby merges sight and sound, blending them together to captivate audiences and tell a deeper story.
