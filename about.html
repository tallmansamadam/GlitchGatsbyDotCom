about the artist

Building off of a lifelong love of jazz and blues, Glitch Gatsby brings together an anachronistic fusion of brass and bass, with live saxophone and visuals.

About ten years into performing, he holds residencies with Detroit's esteemed Theatre Bizarre and Ybor City, Tampa's biannual Steampunk Ball, as well as various spots at Electric Forest music festival, including the speakeasy and silent disco.
